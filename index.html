Common Questions

What most offices
ask us first.

Will this work with my existing practice management software?

Yes. PulseAI integrates natively with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, Curve Dental, and Carestream. For other systems, we use a secure API bridge. We've never encountered a practice we couldn't connect to — setup is handled entirely by our team, not yours.

How long does setup actually take?

Most practices are fully live in 48–72 hours. We do a 30-minute onboarding call, collect your preferences and branding, connect to your PMS, and handle the rest. Your team doesn't need to install anything or manage any technical setup.

Is patient data secure and HIPAA compliant?

Absolutely. PulseAI is fully HIPAA-compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, we sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with every practice, and we never use patient data for training or any purpose outside your automation workflows.

Can I customize the messages patients receive?

Everything is fully customizable — tone, timing, channel (SMS vs. email), content, and language. We start with proven templates built from thousands of dental patient interactions, then tailor them to match your practice's voice before going live.

What does it cost?

Pricing is based on practice size and the automations you need. Most single-location practices invest between $397–$697/month — a fraction of what one no-show or one missed new patient call costs. We'll give you a custom quote after the demo call, with no pressure and no hidden fees.

What if I want to cancel?

Month-to-month contracts only. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice — no lock-ins, no cancellation fees. We keep clients by delivering results, not by trapping them in contracts.
